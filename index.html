About

Hi. My name's Ondřej, some call me Leo. I'm a computer science student (dabbling in research too) based in Brno, Czech Republic.

I'm in the final year of the Information Technology and Artificial Intelligence master's programme at FIT BUT. My specialisation is Computer Networks, I tend to focus on cybersecurity.

I'm a a member of FIT BUT Students' Union where I spent three years as a team leader and half a year as the chairman, leading the association through a successful reorganisation.

I work part-time at Lingea as a full-stack web developer and system administrator.

I speak Czech (and sometimes Slovak), English, limited Spanish and very basic Swedish.

I play the piano and the guitar, I enjoy various genres of music profoundly - listening and performing. My other hobbies include specialty coffee and linguistics.
